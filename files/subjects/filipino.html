Filipino

Filipino subject mainly focus in Philippine literature. This subject tackles literary pieces of famous Filipino writers. Moreover, it also focus in learning the filipino language deeply.

The former teaches basic language constructs such as nouns (pangngalan), pronouns (panghalip), pandiwa (verbs), among others. The latter teaches the literary pieces of famous writers such as Francisco Baltazar where the famous line “hahamakin ang lahat masunod ka lamang”
